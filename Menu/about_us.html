About Us

VEX Robotics

MTHS VEX Robotics is a STEM club at Mountlake Terrace High School. This year will be our third year competing in VEX VRC Robotics competitions.

Teams

Glacier 612A was a 4 student team with 3 freshmen and one sophomore. Their goal was to build a robot that could both score points in towers and stacks so they built a forklift bot that could reach the lowest cup.

Solar Flare 612B was a 4 student team, with 3 freshmen and one junior. Oliver Easton was the team captain. During the 2019-2020 competition season, they built a robot that was able to stack up to 3 cubes and place them in the towers. They were a very new team with lots of freshmen but they did very well.

Inferno 612C, formerly known as Heat Misers, is a girl-powered team (meaning the majority of the members are female). Brooke Rinehimer was the team captain. They made a four-wheel-drive claw bot that could stack up to two cubes in a corner in the 2019-2020 season. They also made a four-wheel-drive robot in the 2018-2019 season that could easily flip the caps and shoot the middle flag. Heat Misers did very well in the 2018-2019 season and made it to VEX State for Washington.

Frostbyte 612X was a 5 member team, with Maximus Lockhart as the team captain. They made a 6 wheel drive robot for the 2019-2020 competition, Tower Takeover. Absolute Zero 612Y was a 5 member team, with Alfredo Alamdar as the team captain. They made a four-wheel-drive robot for the 2019-2020 competition that could easily stack up to six cubes in the corner and had a method of scoring in the towers as well. They did very well in the last season and even made it to VEX State.

Heatwave 612Z was a 5 member team, with 4 freshmen and a sophomore. Annabelle Millis was the team captain. For the 2019-2020 season, they made a robot that could stack a few blocks and could move at a pretty good speed. They did well in most of the competitions even though they were mostly freshmen and new to the club.
