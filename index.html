MTHS VEX Robotics!

Our goal is to provide a place for MTHS STEM students to extend their reach in computer science and engineering

Introduction

MTHS VEX Robotics is a STEM club at Mountlake Terrace High School. Our club focuses on VEX VRC Robotics competitions and other activities and competitions related to robotics. Our club is delightful. Everyone is very kind and will always offer help when needed. Take a look around our website and see the history of events and competitions that we’ve participated in and even how to join if you are located in our district!

Competitions

Our VEX teams compete against other school and REC teams in REC Foundation's Competitions

Competition Description

Our VEX Robotics Competitions are played on a 12’x12’ square field configured as seen below. Two Alliances – one “red” and one “blue” – composed of two Teams each, compete in matches consisting of a fifteen second Autonomous Period, followed by a one minute and forty-five second Driver Controlled Period.
