Our teams compete against other school teams and rec teams in REC Foundation Competitions

Competition Description

Our VEX Robotics Competitions are played on a 12’x12’ square field configured as seen above. Two Alliances – one “red” and one “blue” – composed of two Teams each, compete in matches consisting of a fifteen second Autonomous Period, followed by a one minute and forty-five second Driver Controlled Period.
